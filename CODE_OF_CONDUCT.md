# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects professional, respectful, and technically honest collaboration.

Participants should:

- critique ideas directly without attacking people
- communicate constraints and tradeoffs clearly
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or bad-faith behavior
- respect maintainers' decisions about project scope and repository boundaries

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- personal attacks
- harassment or threats
- discriminatory language or conduct
- deliberately disruptive or dishonest participation
- publishing private information without permission

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this policy or damage the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately through the contact path in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md) until a dedicated conduct address is published.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they judge appropriate, including warning, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from project spaces.
